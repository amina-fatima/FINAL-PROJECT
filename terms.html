By booking an appointment or using our services at Roophause, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully before your visit.

01

Appointments & Bookings

All appointments can be booked via our website, phone, or in person. Bookings are confirmed only upon receiving a confirmation message from us.

  • Please arrive 5‐10 minutes before your scheduled appointment
  • Late arrivals may result in a shortened service or rescheduling
  • Walk-ins are welcome subject to availability

02

Cancellation & No-Show Policy

We understand that plans can change. We kindly ask for advance notice if you need to cancel or reschedule.

Please note: Cancellations made less than 24 hours before your appointment or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee or affect future booking eligibility.

To cancel or reschedule, please contact us as early as possible via phone or our website.

03

Services & Pricing

All service prices are listed on our website and at the salon. Prices are subject to change without prior notice. The final price of your service will be confirmed at the time of your appointment.

  • Additional charges may apply for extra length, thickness, or special treatments
  • We reserve the right to decline services that may cause harm to the client
  • Results may vary based on hair type, condition, and previous treatments

04

Health & Safety

Your safety is our priority. Please inform your stylist of any allergies, scalp conditions, or recent chemical treatments before your service begins. Roophause reserves the right to refuse or modify a service if it poses a risk to the client's health.

Patch tests may be required for certain chemical services such as colouring or perming. We recommend booking a patch test 48 hours in advance.

05

Payments

We accept cash and major digital payment methods. Full payment is due upon completion of your service. We do not offer credit or deferred payment options.

  • Receipts are provided upon request
  • Gift vouchers are non-refundable and non-transferable
  • Promotions and discounts cannot be combined unless stated

06

Refunds & Complaints

We strive to ensure every client leaves satisfied. If you are unhappy with your service, please let us know before leaving the salon. We will do our best to address any concerns at the time of your visit.

Refunds are not provided for completed services. However, we are happy to offer a complimentary adjustment within 7 days of your appointment if the result does not meet the agreed outcome.
