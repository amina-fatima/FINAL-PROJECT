Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services does RoopHaus offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of beauty treatments, including hair styling, professional makeup, skincare facials, manicures, pedicures, waxing, and specialized bridal packages. Whether you visit our salon or request a home visit, you receive the same premium quality.

2. How do I book an appointment with RoopHaus?

Booking is simple! Just fill out the appointment form on our website with your details and preferred services. Once submitted, our team will review your request and contact you shortly via phone or WhatsApp to confirm the time, date, and location.

3. Do you provide services at my home?

Yes! We bring the salon experience to your doorstep. You can enjoy almost all our services in the comfort of your own home. Simply select the "Home Service" option while filling out our booking form.

4. How soon will you contact me after I fill out the form?

We aim to respond to all booking requests as quickly as possible. Generally, you can expect a call or message from our team within 2 to 4 business hours to finalize your appointment.

5. Can I change or cancel my appointment?

We understand that plans change! If you need to reschedule or cancel, please let us know at least 24 hours in advance. You can do this by replying to our confirmation message or calling our support line.

6. What safety and hygiene protocols do you follow?

Your safety is our priority. Our professionals use sanitized tools, disposable kits where applicable, and follow strict hygiene protocols. For home services, our staff arrives with a fresh kit to ensure a clean and safe environment.

7. Do you offer bridal or group bookings?

Absolutely! We love being part of your special occasions. Whether it's for a bride, a bridesmaid, or a group of friends for a party, we offer customized packages. Just mention "Bridal" or "Group Booking" in the notes section of our form.
